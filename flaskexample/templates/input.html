BirdTrek

Welcome to BirdTrek! BirdTrek is a birding trip planner that puts you at the right location at the right time to maximize your chances of seeing all the bird species in your state over one calendar year. BirdTrek bases your trips out of the home location of your choice, giving you weekly intineraries. For each week, you will have a list of between one and five locations. For each location, you receive driving directions and a list of birds to look for.

Currently only locations in Wisconsin will get an itinerary, the whole US coming soon!
