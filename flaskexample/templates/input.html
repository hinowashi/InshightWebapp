BirdWatch

Welcome to BirdWatch! BirdWatch is a birding trip planner that guaratees you will be at the right location at the right time to maximize your chances of seen all the birds in your state. A key feature of BirdWatch is that it uses your home location as your home base of operations, giving you a chance to see all the birds without the need of a road trip. BirdWatch will give you a handfull of recomendations of where to be and what to see for each week. For eBird users it can also plan a trip to see state lifers!
